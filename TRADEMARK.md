## Forks and derivatives

You are free to fork the code (Apache-2.0) and the skill content (CC-BY-4.0)
under their respective license terms. A fork that diverges substantially
should pick a new name; trivial or short-lived forks may keep "Loadout" in
the repo name only with a clear "fork of" notice in the README.

## Vertical packs and paid extensions

The Loadout name is reserved for the official paid tier (vertical skill
packs, hosted wizard, `agent` CLI, team sync). Third parties offering
paid Claude-skill products built on top of Loadout should pick a distinct
brand and clearly attribute Loadout for the underlying framework.
